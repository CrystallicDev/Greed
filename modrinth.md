## Description 

</center>

Greed is a mod that aim to rebalance some vanilla mechanics, targeting Enchanting and Potion Brewing, without going
too far and changing the feel of it.
It affects the available enchantments and curses in the enchanting table, and gives a bigger place to cauldrons in potions brewing. 
These effects are particularly strong when paired with [Cristallite](https://modrinth_cristallite_link), because it reworks Lapis-Lazuli's ore
spawns.

Greed also integrates with [Farmer's Delight](https://modrinth_farmer_delight), to rebalance vanilla food gathering, and food properties,
making Farmer's Delight food a more viable choice.

All of **Greed**'s functionalities can be enbled / disabled via the config file !

##  New Curses

<br>
<br>
</center>

Greed adds **7 new Curses** to the game : 

|Curse Name|Max Level|Description|
|---|---|---|
|Curse of Absorption|1|Can be applied to armor. Absorb experience like Mending.|
|Curse of The Sponge|1|Can be applied to armor. Reduce any incoming positive potion effects durations by 20% to 40%|
|Curse of Creeping|1|Can be applied to boots. Considerably reduce the player sneaking's speed.|
|Curse of Combination|1|Can be applied to any item. Prevent the fusion of this item on an anvil.|
|Curse of Voiding|1|Can be applied to any tool. Works like an inverted Fortune effect.|
|Curse of Scarcity|1|Can be applied to any weapon. Works like an inverted Looting effect.|
|Curse of Lightweight|1|Can be applied to any armor. Increase taken knockback.|

##  Enchanting Table rebalance

<br>
<br>
</center>

Greed rebalances the way the **enchanting table** works, be giving it "tiers" depending on the block below it.

|Block|Effect|
|---|---|
|Any|Books cannot be enchanted. All enchantments are maxed at Level 1. No secondary enchantments can be obtained. Curses have 10% chance to appear on items.|
|Lapis Block|Books can be enchanted. All enchantments are maxed at Level 1. Secondary enchantments can be obtained. Curses have 10% chance to appear on items.|
|Amethyst Cluster|Vanilla functions|


No matter the table's tier, bookshelves will always work, but may not affect enchantments levels depending on the tier. Config options exist
to make enchanting consume the amount of experience showed in the table. _(ex : maxed enchants can cost up to 30 levels)_

##  Brewing & Cauldrons improvements

<br>
<br>
</center>

Greed adds functionality to **cauldrons**, by making them able to hold potions instead of water. Greed also
prevents the player from filling a water bottle in a water source, therefore requiring a cauldron. _(Can be disabled)_
Once a cauldron is filled with potions, it can then be applied to a single bottle, to combine all the effects in one drink !
